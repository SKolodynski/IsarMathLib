section ‚Äπ Alternative definitions of uniformity ‚Ä∫

theory UniformSpace_ZF_2 imports UniformSpace_ZF
begin

text‚Äπ The ‚ÄπUniformSpace_ZF‚Ä∫ theory defines uniform spaces based on entourages (also called surroundings 
  sometimes). In this theory we consider an alternative definition based of the 
  notion of uniform covers.
  ‚Ä∫

subsection‚Äπ Uniform covers ‚Ä∫

text‚Äπ Given a set $X$ we can consider collections of subsets of $X$ whose unions are equal to $X$. 
  Any such collection is called a cover of $X$. We can define relation on the set of covers
  of $X$, called "star refinement" (definition below). A collection of covers is a 
  "family of uniform covers" if it is a filter with respect to the start refinement ordering. 
  The members of such family are called a "uniform cover", but one has to remember that this notion 
  has meaning only in the contexts a the whole family of uniform covers. Looking at a specific cover
  in isolation we can not say whether it is a uniform cover or not.  ‚Ä∫

text‚Äπ The set of all covers of $X$ is called ‚ÄπCovers(X)‚Ä∫. ‚Ä∫

definition 
  "Covers(X) ‚â° {P ‚àà Pow(Pow(X)). ‚ãÉP = X}" 

text‚ÄπA cover of a nonempty set must have a nonempty member.‚Ä∫

lemma cover_nonempty: assumes "X‚â†0" "P ‚àà Covers(X)"
  shows "‚àÉU‚ààP. U‚â†0"
  using assms unfolding Covers_def by blast  

text‚Äπ A "star" of $R$ with respect to $\mathcal{R}$ is the union of all $S\in \mathcal{R}$ that
  intersect $R$. ‚Ä∫

definition
  "Star(U,P) ‚â° ‚ãÉ{V‚ààP. V‚à©U ‚â† 0}"

text‚ÄπAn element of $\mathcal{R}$ is a subset of its star with respect to $\mathcal{R}$. ‚Ä∫

lemma element_subset_star: assumes "U‚ààP" shows "U ‚äÜ Star(U,P)"
  using assms unfolding Star_def by auto

text‚ÄπAn alternative formula for star of a singleton.‚Ä∫

lemma star_singleton: shows "(‚ãÉ{V√óV. V‚ààP})``{x} = Star({x},P)"
  unfolding Star_def by blast

text‚ÄπStar of a larger set is larger.‚Ä∫

lemma star_mono: assumes "U‚äÜV" shows "Star(U,P) ‚äÜ Star(V,P)"
  using assms unfolding Star_def by blast

text‚ÄπIn particular, star of a set is larger than star of any singleton in that set.‚Ä∫

corollary star_single_mono: assumes "x‚ààU" shows "Star({x},P) ‚äÜ Star(U,P)"
  using assms star_mono by auto

text‚ÄπA cover $\mathcal{R}$ (of $X$) is said to be a "barycentric refinement" of a cover $\mathcal{C}$ 
  iff for every $x\in X$ the star of $\{x\}$ in $\mathcal{R}$ is contained 
  in some $C\in \mathcal{C}$. ‚Ä∫

  IsBarycentricRefinement ("_ <‚áßB _" 90) 
  where "P <‚áßB Q ‚â° ‚àÄx‚àà‚ãÉP.‚àÉU‚ààQ.  Star({x},P) ‚äÜ U"

text‚ÄπA cover is a barycentric refinement of the collection of stars of the singletons
  $\{x \}$ as $x$ ranges over $X$.‚Ä∫

  assumes "P ‚àà Covers(X)" shows "P <‚áßB {Star({x},P). x‚ààX}"
  using assms unfolding Covers_def IsBarycentricRefinement_def by blast    

text‚Äπ A cover $\mathcal{R}$ is a "star refinement" of a cover $\mathcal{C}$  iff 
  for each $R\in \mathcal{R}$ there is a $C\in \mathcal{C}$ such that the star of $R$ with 
  respect to $\mathcal{R}$ is contained in $C$. ‚Ä∫

  IsStarRefinement ("_ <‚áß* _" 90)
  where "P <‚áß* Q ‚â° ‚àÄU‚ààP.‚àÉV‚ààQ. Star(U,P) ‚äÜ V"

  using assms unfolding Star_def IsStarRefinement_def Covers_def by auto

text‚ÄπStar refinement implies barycentric refinement. ‚Ä∫

lemma star_is_bary: assumes "Q‚ààCovers(X)" and "Q <‚áß* P"
  shows "Q <‚áßB P"
proof -
  from assms(1) have "‚ãÉQ = X" unfolding Covers_def by simp
  { fix x assume "x‚ààX"
    with ‚Äπ‚ãÉQ = X‚Ä∫ obtain R where "R‚ààQ" and "x‚ààR" by auto
    with assms(2) obtain U where "U‚ààP" and "Star(R,Q) ‚äÜ U"
      unfolding IsStarRefinement_def by auto
    from ‚Äπx‚ààR‚Ä∫ ‚ÄπStar(R,Q) ‚äÜ U‚Ä∫ have "Star({x},Q) ‚äÜ U"
      using star_single_mono by blast
    with ‚ÄπU‚ààP‚Ä∫ have "‚àÉU‚ààP. Star({x},Q) ‚äÜ U" by auto
 } with ‚Äπ‚ãÉQ = X‚Ä∫ show ?thesis unfolding IsBarycentricRefinement_def
    by simp

      with ‚ÄπStar(U,P) = 0‚Ä∫ have "‚àÉV‚ààR. Star(U,P) ‚äÜ V" by auto
    }
    moreover 
    { assume "U‚â†0"
      then obtain x‚á©0 where "x‚á©0‚ààU" by auto
      with assms(1,2,5) ‚ÄπU‚ààP‚Ä∫ obtain V where "V‚ààR" and "Star({x‚á©0},Q) ‚äÜ V"
        unfolding Covers_def IsBarycentricRefinement_def by auto
      have "Star(U,P) ‚äÜ V"
      proof -
        { fix W assume "W‚ààP" and "W‚à©U ‚â† 0"
          from ‚ÄπW‚à©U ‚â† 0‚Ä∫ obtain x where "x‚ààW‚à©U" by auto
          with assms(2) ‚ÄπU‚ààP‚Ä∫ have  "x‚àà‚ãÉP" by auto

          with ‚ÄπU‚ààP‚Ä∫ ‚ÄπW‚ààP‚Ä∫ ‚Äπx‚ààW‚à©U‚Ä∫ ‚Äπx‚á©0‚ààU‚Ä∫ ‚ÄπStar({x‚á©0},Q) ‚äÜ V‚Ä∫ have "W‚äÜV"

text‚ÄπThe notion of a filter defined in ‚ÄπTopology_ZF_4‚Ä∫ is not sufficiently general to use it to 
  define uniform covers, so we write the conditions directly. A nonempty 
  collection $\Theta$ of covers of $X$ is a family of uniform covers if  
  
  a) if $\mathcal{R} \in \Theta$ and $\mathcal{C}$ is any cover of $X$ such that $\mathcal{R}$ is 
  a star refinement of $\mathcal{C}$, then $\mathcal{C} \in \Theta$.
  
  b) For any $\mathcal{C},\mathcal{D} \in \Theta$ there is some $\mathcal{R}\in\Theta$ 
  such that $\mathcal{R}$ is a star refinement of both $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{R}$.

  This departs slightly from the definition in Wikipedia that requires that $\Theta$ contains the
  trivial cover $\{ X\}$. As we show in lemma ‚Äπunicov_contains_trivial‚Ä∫ below we don't loose 
  anything by weakening the definition this way.
  ‚Ä∫

definition
  AreUniformCovers ("_ {are uniform covers of} _" 90)
  where "Œò {are uniform covers of} X ‚â°  Œò ‚äÜ Covers(X) ‚àß Œò‚â†0 ‚àß 
  (‚àÄ‚Ñõ‚ààŒò.‚àÄùíû‚ààCovers(X). ((‚Ñõ <‚áß* ùíû) ‚ü∂ ùíû‚ààŒò)) ‚àß
  (‚àÄùíû‚ààŒò.‚àÄùíü‚ààŒò.‚àÉ‚Ñõ‚ààŒò.(‚Ñõ <‚áß* ùíû) ‚àß (‚Ñõ <‚áß* ùíü))"

text‚ÄπA family of uniform covers contain the trivial cover $\{ X\}$.‚Ä∫

  shows "{X} ‚àà Œò"

  from assms obtain ‚Ñõ where "‚Ñõ‚ààŒò" unfolding AreUniformCovers_def by blast
  with assms show ?thesis using cover_stref_triv

text‚Äπ From the definition of uniform covers we know that if a uniform cover $P$ 
  is a star-refinement of a cover $Q$ then $Q$ is in a uniform cover. The next lemma
  shows that in order for $Q$ to be a uniform cover it is sufficient that $P$ is a 
  barycentric refinement of $Q$. ‚Ä∫

  assumes "Œò {are uniform covers of} X" "P‚ààŒò" "Q ‚àà Covers(X)" "P <‚áßB Q" and "X‚â†0" 
  shows "Q‚ààŒò"

  from assms(1,2,3) ‚ÄπR‚ààŒò‚Ä∫ have 
    "P ‚àà Covers(X)" "Q ‚àà Covers(X)" "R ‚àà Covers(X)"
    unfolding AreUniformCovers_def by auto
  with assms(1,3,4,5) ‚ÄπR‚ààŒò‚Ä∫ ‚ÄπR <‚áßB P‚Ä∫ show ?thesis 
    using bary_bary_star unfolding AreUniformCovers_def by auto

    with I obtain S where "y‚ààS" "x‚ààS" "S ‚àà {W``{t}. t‚ààX}" by auto
    from ‚ÄπS ‚àà {W``{t}. t‚ààX}‚Ä∫ obtain t where "t‚ààX" and "S = W``{t}" 
      by auto
    with ‚Äπx‚ààS‚Ä∫ ‚Äπy‚ààS‚Ä∫ have "‚ü®t,x‚ü© ‚àà W" and "‚ü®t,y‚ü© ‚àà W"

    then obtain t where "‚ü®x,t‚ü© ‚àà W" and "‚ü®t,y‚ü© ‚àà W"

    from assms(2) ‚Äπ‚ü®t,y‚ü© ‚àà W‚Ä∫ have "t‚ààX" by auto
    from ‚Äπ‚ü®x,t‚ü© ‚àà W‚Ä∫ have "‚ü®t,x‚ü© ‚àà converse(W)" by auto
    with assms(1) I ‚Äπ‚ü®t,y‚ü© ‚àà W‚Ä∫ ‚Äπt‚ààX‚Ä∫ have "y ‚àà Star({x},{W``{t}. t‚ààX})"
