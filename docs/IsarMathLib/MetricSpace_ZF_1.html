  context, but that is in turn an extension of the ‚Äπloop1‚Ä∫ locale that defines notation

subsection‚ÄπMetric spaces are Hausdorff as topological spaces‚Ä∫

text‚ÄπThe usual (real-valued) metric spaces are a special case of ordered loop valued
  metric spaces defined in the ‚ÄπMetricSpace_ZF‚Ä∫ theory, hence they are $T_2$ 
  as topological spaces. ‚Ä∫

  the set of open balls is the base of that topology and the carrier of the
  topology is the underlying (p)metric space carrier $X$. ‚Ä∫

  using rord_down_directs pmetric_space_pmetric_space1_valid
